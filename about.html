About Me

My name is Shahreen Hussain, and I am a Bengali-Indian American. I have roots in both Bangladesh, and in India, and am therefore familiar with the various dishes of both cultures.

A specialty of anyone from the Indian Subcontinent, is the variety of dishes that exist, and the variety of flavor that can be added.

This site will contain curry dishes, sweet dishes, as well as sweet drinks from the Indo-Bengali Culture, as well as describe when and why these dishes are made.
