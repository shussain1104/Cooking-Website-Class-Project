If You Want that Indian Taste, Be Prepared for the Spice

Namaskar! Sastriyakal! Adaab! Here we want to expose your tastebuds to the spices and flavours of India. From spicy and savoury curries to sweet and fruitful desserts, this website will give some of the more popular dishes in both Indian and Bengali cultures and subcultures.

What to Look For on This Site

This site will not only contain recipes from the many subcultures in the Indian Subcontinent, but will also describe the various occasions when these delicacies are made, as well as why I chose to share these recipes.
